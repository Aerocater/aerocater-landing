Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data AeroCater collects, why, and what rights you have over it — whether you're a dispatcher, driver, supervisor, or admin using the Service.

1. Who we are

AeroCater Inc. ("we," "us") provides dispatch operations software for aviation catering companies. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

Privacy contact: privacy@aerocater.ca

2. What we collect

CategoryExamplesCollected from
Account dataName, email, role, organizationYou, at signup
Driver scan dataTimestamps, trolley/truck IDs, location of scanDriver mobile app
Dispatch logsFlight assignments, gate/truck pairings, SLA timersSystem-generated during use
GPS/location dataTruck location during active dispatchDriver device, if enabled
Trolley trackingChain-of-custody scans, hydraulic cycle countsDriver mobile app, fleet sensors
Shift dataSchedules, staff assignmentsAdmin input
Login/audit dataLogin timestamps, IP address, action logsAutomatically, on use

We don't collect payment card data directly — that's handled by our payment processor under their own privacy terms.

3. Why we process this data

  • Contract performance — operating the dispatch platform you've signed up for requires processing dispatch, scan, and shift data
  • Legitimate interest — audit logs and login data support security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Consent — where required (optional analytics, marketing communications), we ask first
  • Legal obligation — retention of certain records is required by aviation and food-safety regulation

4. Retention

  • Dispatch and scan logs: retained 7 years, to support food-safety chain-of-custody requirements
  • Account and login data: retained for the life of the account, plus 90 days after closure
  • GPS/location data: retained 12 months

5. Who we share data with

We don't sell personal data. We share it with sub-processors who help us run the Service (see our Sub-processor List), with your organization's other authorized users as intended by the Service's design (dispatchers see driver activity within their own station only — never across organizations), and with legal authorities if required by law or to protect safety.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, correct inaccurate data, request deletion (subject to the retention obligations above), receive your data in a portable format, and object to certain processing.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@aerocater.ca. We respond within 30 days, as required under GDPR and similar frameworks.

7. International transfers

Our infrastructure (Supabase) is hosted in the United States (AWS). If you're located in the EU/UK, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses to safeguard transfers of your data outside those regions.

8. Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for details on what cookies we use and how to manage your preferences.

9. Children's privacy

The Service isn't directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

10. Changes to this policy

We'll notify account administrators by email of material changes at least 30 days before they take effect.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or rights requests: privacy@aerocater.ca
